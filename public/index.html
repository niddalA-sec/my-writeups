Hi, I’m niddalA 🚩

MTA Student | MSEC Member | Pwnable Enthusiast

Welcome to my digital garden. I am currently a student at the Military Technical Academy (MTA) and a core member of the MTA Security Club (MSEC).

This blog serves as a technical repository for my CTF journey, with a primary focus on Pwn (Binary Exploitation). I enjoy diving deep into low-level systems, analyzing assembly code, and bypassing modern exploit mitigations like Stack Canary, ASLR, and NX to build reliable ROP chains.

Exploit to understand, understand to protect.
